## §7 Statistical Methodology

> **Convention-freeze note.** This chapter is initialised as a Phase-2 prerequisite. Its outline mirrors Phase-2 Projektantrag §§8.1 and 10. The chapter is intentionally a **stub at v1.0 freeze**: methodology language is captured here only to the extent that it is already pre-registered in the Projektantrag. Detailed procedure write-ups are deferred to the Architect handover that opens the WP-α₀ draft (Projektantrag §13 handshake).

### §7.1 Monte Carlo null generation under estimated parameters

*Outline (full prose deferred to WP-α₀ opening).*

- Per-noise-family null distributions are generated by Monte Carlo simulation rather than read from asymptotic tables. Asymptotic critical values for goodness-of-fit tests assume known distributional parameters; estimated parameters invalidate them ([Lilliefors 1967](projektantrag_p2.md#14-references-external-coastlines-citation-only) is cited in the Projektantrag for the precedent).
- Anderson–Darling test statistics are computed against Monte-Carlo-derived null distributions, not against `scipy.stats.anderson`'s asymptotic critical values.
- Replicate count *R*, generator family, and seed-derivation protocol are pre-registered in [Projektantrag §10](projektantrag_p2.md#10-rng-governance-frozen).

### §7.2 Dual materiality threshold (T1 vs T2 escalation)

*Outline (full prose deferred to WP-α₀ opening).*

Phase-2 §8.1.1 freezes:

- Statistical threshold: AD-test p-value below pre-registered α (suggested α = 0.01).
- Effect-size threshold: Kolmogorov distance Δ_max > 0.05 **or** tail-ratio deviation > 20 % at the 95th-percentile reference.
- Both must trigger to declare a shape change. Exactly-one triggers produces a sensitivity-analysis appendix and a caveat range, not a single-point threshold.

The verbal justification of the four numerical choices (α, Δ_max, tail-ratio, Spearman-ρ floor) is held in [Projektantrag §8.1.1](projektantrag_p2.md#811-dual-materiality-threshold-for-t1-vs-t2). Empirical tuning during WP-α₀ requires a convention-freeze amendment.

### §7.3 Spearman-ρ ordering floor (T2 vs T3 escalation)

*Outline (full prose deferred to WP-α₀ opening).*

- Per-reading IC ranks under each candidate noise family are paired against the Gaussian-null reference by Spearman correlation on a Monte-Carlo draw.
- Pre-registered floor: ρ ≥ 0.85 (Phase-2 §8.1.2).
- Floor failure escalates to T3 (chart transition). The chart-transition triad (invariant preserved / transformed representation / information gained-or-lost) is logged at the moment of escalation per Phase-2 §8.1.3.

### §7.4 RNG governance (cross-reference)

This handbook does not duplicate the RNG governance content. The authoritative location is [Projektantrag §10](projektantrag_p2.md#10-rng-governance-frozen). The pins relevant to §7 procedures:

- Generator: `numpy.random.default_rng` (PCG64).
- Master seed S₀ from `secrets.randbits(64)`, logged at campaign start.
- Per-stream seed: `SHA-256(S₀ ‖ family_name ‖ str(replicate) ‖ scenario_id)[:8] → uint64`.
- Replicate count for WP-α₀ Monte Carlo nulls: **R = 10⁴** (pre-registered).
- Bit-identical replay mandate on identical software stack (numpy ≥ 2.0, CPython hashlib SHA-256, little-endian).
